Guitar Songs

Why learning them?

Because it's fun. Often easier than creating one. Moreover, the guitar songs you will know will accumulate one after the other, and soon enough, it'll become even more fun.

What do you have to do? Simply follow the guide. You can even choose between reading and watching to learn a song. No more chit-chat. Let's get to it!

1. Acoustic Guitar Tunes.

Got an acoustic guitar? This section is for you.

For your comfort, every song will be uploaded with the difficulty level. You can also choose between watching the video, or reading the lesson. And a tab is attached, so you can easily follow. Or even learn the song without any help.

  • [Beginner] Milow - You and Me(In My Pocket)
  • [Beginner/Intermediate] Milow - Ayo Technology
  • [Beginner/Intermediate] Milow - You Don't Know
  • [Beginner/Intermediate] Placebo - Protect Me From What I Want
  • [Intermediate] Placebo - Every You Every Me

2. Electric Guitar Tunes.

Wanna learn some songs on the electric guitar? Stay right here.

Every song will be uploaded with the difficulty level to make you feel more comfortable. You can also choose between watching the video, or reading the lesson. And a tab is attached, so you can easily follow. Or even learn the song without any help.

3. Any suggestions?

Want to see a guitar lesson on your favorite song? It's possible. Get in touch with me via the web form. I will answer your request as soon as I can. Note that it's also important to give me the name of the artist. There are a lot of songs with the same name out there.

4. Help on guitar songs

Sometimes, the song gives you a rough time and even after practicing a lot on it, it's just... too hard. At this point, you might want to give up. One word of advice: don't. Or let it rest, just for a few days. Work on something different, but always come back to it.

This moment is called a plateau. It's normal to reach one at some point. But know that, if you practice regularly, there's always a rising slope afterwards.

5. Directions from here.

Depending on your level, you might want to take some lessons to improve yourself. That's necessary when you can't play a song because you cannot reproduce the technique. Take a look at the following page and see if you can find what you need:

  • Beginners guitar lessons
  • Guitar music theory (or general music theory)

Have fun with all this stuff!
